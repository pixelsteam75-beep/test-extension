# #75HER Project: Pixel Digital Wellness Tracker

For the ever-busy tech girlies (like us) to mindfully track their work in the digital realm — and their rest from it.  

Set Pomodoro sessions, receive audible rest cues, and track time spent online in one place with a single Chrome extension.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

**Who:**  
Women in tech juggling multiple digital tasks.

**Problem:**  
Women are disproportionately affected by digital fatigue. One NIMH study shows incidence for anxiety disorders in females at **23.4%**, higher than the **14.3% incidence in males**.

**Impact:**  
Lost time, reduced focus, and decreased quality of life due to task switching and digital overstimulation.

---

# ✨ Solution Overview

**What we built:**  
A holistic productivity tool that encourages mindful productivity without restricting access to the digital tools users need.

Pixel combines **Pomodoro timers, activity monitoring, and breathwork prompts** to encourage users to pause at regular intervals while rewarding focus with affirmations.

### Key Features

**Activity Tracking**  
Automatically tracks time spent on each domain while browsing.

**Pomodoro Timer**  
Focus sessions (25 min) and breaks (5 min) with audible cues.

**Audio Cues**  
Hear a cue when it’s time to focus or pause.

**Breathwork Animations**  
Guided animations help transform Pomodoro breaks into mindful moments.

**Dashboard**  
View:

- Total tracked time today  
- Completed focus sessions  
- Top 10 domains visited  

**100% Local**

All data is stored in **Chrome's local storage** — no accounts, no cloud sync required.
